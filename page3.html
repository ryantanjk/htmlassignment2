    • TRAINING DRILLS AND SKILLS

    JUGGLING

    Keeping the ball up in the air

    Juggling: A skill in football that helps develop a relationship with the ball, improve timing, spatial understanding, touch, motor coordination, body control, balance and leg strength. Mastering this skill will definitely make you a better footballer!

    BALL CONTROL

    Controlling the ball in tight spaces

    Controlling the ball in tight spaces, close quarters with the inside/outside/sole of your feet will bring your dribbling to the next level!

    SHOOTING

    Putting the ball into the back of the net

    In football, the aim is to always score more than the opposition. Practice one touch shooting with me for you to score more and help bring your team to victory!

    PENALTIES

    Be flawless and composed from 12 yards!

    Penalties can make or break a game. Practice them with me to ensure that when the situation arises, you are ready to help your team from the spot!
